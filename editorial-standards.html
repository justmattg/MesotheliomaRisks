Editorial Standards

Purpose

This site is a neutral educational publisher-style resource about mesothelioma and asbestos exposure.

Required content rules

  • Informational only
  • Neutral tone
  • Source-backed
  • No legal direction
  • No medical direction
  • No compensation framing
  • No provider or attorney referral

Forbidden phrases

  • You may qualify
  • File a claim
  • Contact a lawyer
  • Free case review
  • Compensation
  • Settlement
  • Payout
  • Trust fund eligibility

Source hierarchy

  1. Government sources: CDC, EPA, OSHA, NCI
  2. Major nonprofit health sources: American Cancer Society, Mayo Clinic
  3. Primary public-health or oncology publications

Maintenance rule

Prefer fewer, stronger articles over volume.

Editorial integrity

Publisher-style informational resource. No forms. No legal routing. No monetization in this package.

Integrity

Our editorial standards are designed to ensure that the information provided remains objective and free from commercial or legal influence.
