# Editorial Standards

## Purpose
This site is a neutral educational publisher-style resource about mesothelioma and asbestos exposure.

## Required content rules
- Informational only
- Neutral tone
- Source-backed
- No legal direction
- No medical direction
- No compensation framing
- No provider or attorney referral

## Forbidden phrases
- You may qualify
- File a claim
- Contact a lawyer
- Free case review
- Compensation
- Settlement
- Payout
- Trust fund eligibility

## Source hierarchy
1. Government sources: CDC, EPA, OSHA, NCI
2. Major nonprofit health sources: American Cancer Society, Mayo Clinic
3. Primary public-health or oncology publications

## Maintenance rule
Prefer fewer, stronger articles over volume.
