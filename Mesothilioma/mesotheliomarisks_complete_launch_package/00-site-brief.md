## mesotheliomarisks.com — Website Brief + Architecture

Project direction:
Build mesotheliomarisks.com as a premium informational authority site focused on mesothelioma, asbestos exposure, occupational risk, latency, and public regulatory history.

Primary purpose:
1. Build topical authority and clean organic traffic
2. Maintain a clean regulatory footprint
3. Increase resale value over time

Non-negotiable rules:
- No forms
- No lawyer links
- No lead routing
- No affiliate offers
- No compensation language
- No email capture
- No trust fund / settlement / payout framing

Design direction:
Medical authority + luxury editorial

Launch scope:
- Homepage
- About
- Editorial Standards
- Disclaimer
- Privacy
- 10 live articles
- 5 future articles stored separately for slow release
- robots.txt
- sitemap.xml

Publishing cadence:
Launch with the live set. Publish future articles one at a time every few months.
