Asbestos in Consumer Products and Legacy Materials

A narrow explainer on older materials and products that historically used asbestos and why legacy materials still matter.

Updated March 11, 2026 5 min read Stored separately for later publication.

Asbestos appeared in insulation, flooring, cement products, friction materials, and some industrial and consumer products. The modern issue is mostly not widespread new use but lingering exposure through older materials, renovation, demolition, and improperly managed legacy products. NCI EPA
